Signing

Built-in e-signing of fee agreements and other documents, with mail-merge. You can even charge a retainer or costs deposit with Stripe integration. Clients have secure access to a Leverage-powered client portal.

  • Prospects Website
  • Automated Screening & Interviewing
  • eSigning of Agreements, etc.
  • Optional Payment Processing

Communication

Maximize client engagement with flexible, automated communications, including secure private video blogging to help clients feel connected, personalized emails and text messaging to one client or defined groups of clients, secure document exchange, and robust, intelligent, interactive surveys.

  • Private Client Website
  • Blogging with Video Support
  • Personalized Mass Email & Texting
  • Secure Document Exchange

Management & Organization

Organize your clients with simple or complex queries against all database fields, including client-entered data and completion statuses of surveys and e-signature documents. Automate client communications and tasks with Workflows---like an employee who executes your instructions perfectly each down.

Download client documents and export Leverage-captured data to pre-formatted spreadsheets as needed.

Complete client census and written discovery responses with nearly fully automated tools.

  • Client Detail Pages
  • Automated Client Grouping
  • Custom Tables & Exports
  • Task Management
